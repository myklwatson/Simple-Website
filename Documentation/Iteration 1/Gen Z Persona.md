## Goals and Motivations

- Find stylish, affordable clothing and accessories that feel unique and on-trend.   
- Support brands that are sustainable, cruelty-free, and transparent about their values.   
- Save time by discovering new products quickly through social media recommendations.   
- Earn rewards or discounts through loyalty programs and student discounts.   

---

## Frustrations

- Inconsistent sizing and poor product photos that do not reflect real life.   
- Slow delivery times and lack of tracking updates.   
- Complicated returns processes that require printing labels or paying for return postage.   
- Shipping costs shown only at the last steps of checkout.   

---

## Challenges and Obstacles

- Limited budget as a student, compares prices and waits for sales.   
- Feels overwhelmed by too many options and long product pages.   
- Distrusts brands with poor reviews, unclear return policies, or hidden fees at checkout.   
- Frustration when sites are slow, not mobile-friendly, or require account creation before browsing.
