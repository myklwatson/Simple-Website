## Goals and Motivations

- Wants a simple way to shop from home

- Likes being able to view a wide range of products easily  
- Wants to avoid the rush of physical stores  
- Wants to shop independently (without needing help)

- Constant pop-ups  
- Being forced to create an account before buying

- Cluttered websites  
- Too many steps to buy products  
- Difficult navigation
